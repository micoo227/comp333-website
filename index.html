Frequently-Asked Questions

Q: What is Vybe?
A: Vybe is a platform that allows users to rate, review, and discuss songs all on one website. Vybe also offers tiered subscription plans which provide additional cutting-edge technologies at a low cost.
Q: What is Predictive Rating Technology?
A: Predictive Rating Technology predicts how you will rate a certain song based on past responses of both yourself and other people. This is performed in real-time and is always updating to provide the most accurate prediction.
Q: Is Vybe free to use?
A: Yes, everyone has access to the default features of Vybe (i.e. the ability to rate, review, and discuss songs). However, users can opt into the Musician and Virtuoso Plans to obtain exclusive benefits.
Q: What are some upcoming features of Vybe?
A: We have a lot in store for the users of Vybe, such as access to livestreamed performances and the Vybe Prescriber, which recommends songs based on your previous ratings. Stay tuned for more updates!
